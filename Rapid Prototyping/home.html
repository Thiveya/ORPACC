All employers with employees who work in Ontario are required to complete the Employer Verification process for the ORPP.

Have ready the following information to complete the process: Employer contact information such as address, phone, and email address; number of employees;

  • If there is an existing pension plan for employees, the plan type, plan name, plan registration number, jurisdiction where plan is registered, benefit information
  • The actuary or plan administrator that will confirm the pension plan information
  • The signing officer that will attest to the information

If at any time you are unsure of how to proceed, save your information and contact the ORPP at ClientServices@orpp.ca.
